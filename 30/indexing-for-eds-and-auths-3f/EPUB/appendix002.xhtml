Information Today, Inc. publishes a series of books on indexing in conjunction with the American Society of Indexers. The series covers information specific to running an indexing business, as well as a series of books on various indexing specialties such as history, law, medicine, and scholarly books. Information on the series is available at: books.infotoday.com/books/index.shtml#index. Of particular interest to authors, editors, and others who work with indexers are two chapters from Indexing Specialties: Scholarly Books (ed. Towery & Zafran, ITI 2005): Chapter 8, “Confessions of an Author-Indexer” by John Bealle, and Chapter 9, “The Quality of a Scholarly Index: A Contribution to the Discourse” by Margie Towery.
